I am

Hi! My name is Leo and my favorite way to create is by mixing engineering with storytelling to create impactful projects that resonate with emotion. I think the most interesting projects are the ones where everyone brings a little bit of their creative side. This turns the project into a unique collage of diverse ideas and perspectives and creates something that I think is very meaningful.

I plan on majoring in Electrical Engineering at The University of California, San Diego this fall while also hopefully seeking a duel major in applied math, biology, or physics.
